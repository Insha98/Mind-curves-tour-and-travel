Top Destinations

Embark on a journey through the most breathtaking corners of the valley. From tranquil waters to snow-capped peaks, discover the places that make Kashmir truly the 'Paradise on Earth'.

Dal Lake

The heartbeat of Srinagar, Dal Lake is more than just water; it's a floating ecosystem. Experience the legendary Shikara rides, stay in intricate Victorian-style houseboats, and witness the sunrise at the vibrant floating vegetable market.

Gulmarg

Rightfully called the 'Meadow of Flowers', Gulmarg is a premier winter sports destination. It boasts the world's second-highest operating cable car, taking you to the clouds at Apharwat Peak.

Pahalgam

The 'Valley of Shepherds' is a serene retreat where the roaring Lidder River meets pine forests. Explore the picturesque Betaab Valley or trek to the stunning Aru Valley.

Sonamarg

Known as the 'Meadow of Gold', Sonamarg is home to the spectacular Thajiwas Glacier. Surrounded by towering Himalayan peaks, it serves as the base for legendary treks.
